Crab Salad

Description

Mmmmm!Yummy! You always wanted to make crab salad for your family? What are you waiting for?

Ingredients

  • 1 pound imitation crab meat, flaked
  • ½ cup finely chopped celery, or more to taste
  • ½ cup reduced-fat ranch dressing (such as Hidden Valley Ranch® LIght)
  • ⅓ cup mayonnaise (such as Hellmann's®/Best Foods®)
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon minced fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Gently mix flaked imitation crab, celery, ranch dressing, mayonnaise, sugar, and parsley in a salad bowl until thoroughly combined. Refrigerate overnight, stirring occasionally. Stir again just before serving.
